FAQ

Common questions

How fast can I get an appointment?

Most bookings are confirmed within 4 business hours. Green or cloudy water requests are prioritized — often same-day or next-day on the West Side and North Shore.

Do you service hot tubs only?

Yes. PureCare Spa runs year-round on weekly or bi-weekly plans. Many clients add spa care to their pool plan with our Combo package.

Are chemicals included in the monthly price?

Yes — all PureCare plans include chemicals up to a fair-use allowance per visit. If your pool needs unusually heavy treatment (e.g. after a storm or neglect), we'll discuss any overage before proceeding.

What if my pool turns green?

Our Pure Recovery service handles cloudy and green water at flat-fee pricing starting at $295. PureCare members get the trip fee waived. We include a follow-up check within 72 hours.

Do I need to be home for service?

No. After the first visit, gate codes or lockbox access work perfectly. We'll note your access details when we confirm your booking.

When should I book my spring opening?

March through April is ideal. Book early — especially before March 15 — to secure your preferred slot and the $895 open + close bundle rate.

Do you handle equipment repairs?

Minor repairs like seals and O-rings, yes. Major electrical or gas heater work is referred to licensed partners we trust. We'll always diagnose honestly.

Do you service strata or commercial pools?

Our launch focus is residential. If you manage a strata or commercial property, join our waitlist — we're expanding in Phase 2.
